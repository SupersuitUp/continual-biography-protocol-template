# projections/

One brief per target artifact. The corpus is the asset; everything here is a projection of it, regeneratable as long as the captures exist. Copy [`_TEMPLATE-projection-brief.md`](./_TEMPLATE-projection-brief.md) per artifact.

Briefs accumulate continuously: during every processing pass, events and quotes that clearly serve an artifact get tagged into its brief (a paintable image, a cinematic scene, a chapter seed). When the artifact is finally built, its raw material is already gathered; no fresh trawl of the corpus required.

Known projection recipes elsewhere: [Generate a Microautobiography](https://appliedai.wiki/playbooks/generate-a-microautobiography) for the narrated one-page life story. Memoir manuscripts, children's books, films, and art series follow the same rule: build from the corpus, never instead of it.
